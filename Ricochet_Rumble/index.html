Ricochet Rumble

Player 1's Turn Time: 30s

Pieces:

  • Ricochet: Moves in straight lines, bounces off walls
  • Jumper: Moves in L-shapes (like knight in chess)
  • Slither: Moves diagonally, can slide multiple squares
  • Pounder: Moves orthogonally, can crush adjacent pieces
  • Teleport: Can teleport to any empty square
